Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Introduction

SaveTok ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data.

SaveTok operates entirely on your device. We do not have servers, accounts, or any backend infrastructure that collects or stores your data.

Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Downloaded video files
  • Video metadata (author name, description)
  • App preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Third-Party Services

SaveTok uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple StoreKit — for processing in-app subscriptions. Payments are handled entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.
  • TikTok — the app accesses publicly available TikTok content to download videos at your request.

Analytics

We do not use any analytics or tracking tools. We do not collect usage data, crash reports, or any other telemetry.

Children's Privacy

SaveTok is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via email.
